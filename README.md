### Article

This synthesis is based on the article **The Rhythm In Anything: audio-prompted drums generation with masked language modeling**. \
Written by *Patrick O’Reilly*, *Julia Barnett*, *Hugo Flores Garcia*, *Annie Chu*, *Nathan Pruyne*, *Prem Seetharaman*, *Bryan Pardo*. \
This work was published on September the 19th, 2025, and was presented at the ISMIR Conference 2025.
